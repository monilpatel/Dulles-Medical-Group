Dr. Anil Patel,MD

Dulles Medical Group

About

Dr. Anil Patel has been practicing since 1990 with a specialization in Internal Medicine. He served as the medical director for Heritage Hall Nursing and Rehabilitation from 1993 to 2017 and is now a senior consultant. From 1998 to 2002 he was the Chief of Internal Medicine at Inova Loudoun Hospital. He is also a senior airman medical examiner performing physicals for commercial pilots and air traffic controllers.

Services

Services: Internal Medicine, Geriatric Medicine

Procedures: EKG, lung function tests, hearing and vision test, urine test, Echocardiogram, Carotid Doppler

Conditions Treated: We perform all preventive care including blood tests, cancer screenings and genetic disease tests. All kinds of adult acute sickness and chronic conditions such as Diabetes, high blood pressure, heart disease and arthritis

Occupational Physicals: Pilot and Air Traffic Controller
